About SHAMS Market

Welcome to SHAMS Market, where traditional Moroccan crochet techniques meet modern style. Our skilled crochetiers are dedicated to preserving and advancing the art of Moroccan crochet work. Each bag tells a unique story, intricately crafted with love and precision.

Our artisanship is inspired by the rich cultural heritage of Morocco, blending traditional techniques with contemporary designs. The result is a collection of unique and stylish bags that reflect the beauty and craftsmanship of Moroccan crochetier work.
